Every lookup queries the Omega Ledger directly — nothing here is cached or pre-rendered. The server recomputes the OM109 fingerprint from the token's signature components on every request. This page then performs that same SHA-256 recomputation a second time, independently, using your browser's own crypto engine. Two separate calculations, agreeing, is the proof — not a badge someone asked you to trust.
